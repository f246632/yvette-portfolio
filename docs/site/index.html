About the Artist

Photo: Jurica Dakkar Galeković

Croatian multidisciplinary artist with a Master's degree in Sculpture, focusing on exploring identity and societal expectations through multiple artistic forms.

Her multidisciplinary approach explores identity, tradition, and the position of women in contemporary culture through various mediums including sculpture and painting.
